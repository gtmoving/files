Moving Guidelines

1. For a free one-time quote, send images via phone call, text message, or email
2. Timing starts when the vehicle arrives at the specified location. We do not delay without reason.
If the service extends beyond 2 hours, charges are calculated every half hour.
3. Please inform us in advance if there are valuable items such as large appliances, pianos, glass cabinets, marble countertops, etc.
An extra fee will be charged for these items.
4. Our company can provide boxes and protective film for packing, which will incur an additional charge.
5. During the moving process, if disassembly and reassembly of furniture and appliances like bed frames, tables, shelves, treadmills, washing machines, etc. are required, these services are free and will not incur any additional charges.
6. Empty vehicle charges, which are gasoline supplements, are added based on the moving distance.
